Our Mission

The Giving Shelf is a nonprofit organization dedicated to increasing access to books for underserved communities in the Metro Detroit area. We believe that everyone deserves the joy, knowledge, and opportunity that books provide.

What We Do

  • Collect and distribute books to local shelters, schools, and community centers.
  • Host free book fairs and literacy events.
  • Partner with local libraries and bookstores to expand our reach.
  • Provide mobile book shelves to areas with limited access to reading materials.

Get Involved

Whether you're looking to donate books, volunteer your time, or support us financially, there are many ways to help.

  • Donate new or gently used books.
  • Volunteer at distribution events or book drives.
  • Spread the word about The Giving Shelf in your community.
  • Make a monetary donation to support our efforts.
